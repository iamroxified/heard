How We Work


Framework for Managing Speakers — Step by Step
Discovery & Brief — We begin with a detailed conversation about your event, your audience, your objectives, and the kind of speaker experience you want to create. We ask the questions most clients don't think to ask, so the brief is complete before we begin sourcing.
Sourcing & Shortlist — Drawing on our curated roster and wider network, we present a shortlisted selection of speakers matched to your brief. Each recommendation comes with a rationale — not just a bio and a fee.
Vetting & Confirmation — We handle reference checks, availability confirmation, and initial fee negotiations, ensuring you only move forward with speakers who are the right fit in every dimension.
Contract & Logistics — We manage all contracting, travel arrangements, accommodation coordination, and logistics — so your team is free to focus on the event itself.
Speaker Preparation — Every speaker receives a bespoke pre-event brief from our team, covering audience profile, session objectives, programme context, and any specific requirements. Where needed, we provide coaching and rehearsal support.
On-Site Management — On the day, our team provides on-site speaker coordination — from arrival to green room to stage — ensuring timing, technical, and AV requirements are all managed without the event team carrying the load.
Post-Event Follow-Through — We collect speaker and audience feedback, manage any outstanding contractual requirements, and provide a post-event debrief to inform future programming.

Conference Programme Design — 3 Steps
Step 1: Programme Strategy
We begin by understanding what you want the audience to think, feel, and do differently by the time the event closes. From that objective, we work backwards to build a programme architecture — identifying the themes, the sequence, the session formats, and the speaker types that will move the room from where it starts to where you want it to end.
Step 2: Session Design & Speaker Matching
With the architecture agreed, we design each session individually — crafting the topic framing, the conversation structure, the format (keynote, panel, fireside, provocation, workshop), and the speaker brief. We then source and match speakers to each session, not just to the event, ensuring every voice serves a specific purpose in the programme.
Step 3: Execution & On-Site Delivery
We produce speaker briefs, manage rehearsals, coordinate with your technical and AV team, and provide on-site programme management to ensure the day runs with the precision the design deserves. We are present from the first session to the final close.

Booking Process — How Clients Book Speakers
Submit a booking enquiry via our contact form or book a discovery call directly.
We schedule a 30-minute Discovery Call to understand your event, audience, and speaker requirements.
Within 5 working days, we provide a curated Speaker Shortlist with recommendations, rationale, fees, and availability.
Client selects preferred speaker(s) and we proceed to confirmation, contracting, and logistics.
Speaker preparation and on-site management begins, with a dedicated point of contact throughout.
